Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is like giving computers a brain. It's about making machines think and act like humans. Imagine a robot that can chat with you, play games, or even drive a car all on its own. AI uses data and algorithms to learn and solve problems, just like how we learn from our experiences. It can recognize faces, understand speech, and even make decisions. AI is super helpful in many things like virtual assistants (like Siri or Alexa), medical diagnoses, and self-driving cars. It's all about making machines smart and helping them do tasks that usually need human thinking.
